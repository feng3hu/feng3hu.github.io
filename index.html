Feng Hu (胡枫)

I am a Professor of Economics at University of Science and Technology Beijing, School of Economics and Management. My research interests are in labor, education, entrepreneurship, and economics of science. I received my Ph.D from Guanghua School of Management at Peking University in 2007, and my Bachelor degree from School of Chemistry at Nankai University in 1999. I was a visiting scholar at Economics Department of Brown University and Teachers College, Columbia University.

My work has appeared in leading science and economics journals such as Nature Human Behaviour and Review of Economics and Statistics . Read more about my research.
